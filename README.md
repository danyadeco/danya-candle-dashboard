#  DANYA Candle Dashboard

A Streamlit dashboard for managing candle inventory, sales and profit.

---

## Version 1.0 - First Release

### Features

- Add Product
  Add new candle products with sale price, cost price and stock quantity.

- View Product List
  Display all products stored in the inventory database.

- Update Product
  Edit product prices and stock levels.

- Delete Product
  Remove products from the database.

- Record Sales
  Register new sales transactions.

- Automatic Stock Reduction
  Automatically decrease inventory after each sale.

- Profit Calculation
  Calculate profit based on sale price and cost price.

- Inventory Tracking
  Monitor available stock for all products.

- Business Dashboard Metrics
  Display key business metrics for inventory and sales management.


---
## Version 1.1 - Sales Analytics Update

### Features

- Sale History
  View all recorded sales with product details and sale dates.

- Revenue Dashboard
  Display total revenue, total cost and total profit.

- Best Seller
  Show the best-selling product based on sales quantity.

- Low Stock Alert
  Warn when product inventory becomes low.

- Sales Charts
